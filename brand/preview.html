Logo

The Balaka logo consists of the B mark (a stylized letter B) and the wordmark "Balaka" in Plus Jakarta Sans SemiBold. They can be used together (full logo) or the B mark alone (favicon, compact UI).

Clear Space & Misuse

Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the B mark's upper counter (approximately 25% of the mark height).
